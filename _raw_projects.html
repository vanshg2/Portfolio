Overview

The Recruitment CRM System is a comprehensive web-based platform developed to streamline the recruitment workflow for HR consultancies and staffing agencies. The application centralizes candidate management, recruiter operations, client interactions, and business analytics into a single, secure platform.

The Recruitment CRM was designed to solve these challenges by providing recruiters with an intuitive dashboard for managing candidates, tracking recruitment pipelines, maintaining client records, generating business insights, and automating repetitive administrative tasks.

Unlike traditional recommendation systems that simply suggest similar movies, CineSense provides a complete movie exploration experience. Users can discover similar movies, watch official trailers, analyze audience reviews through Natural Language Processing (NLP), explore cast members, and browse an actor's most popular works—all within a single application.

The platform combines Machine Learning, Natural Language Processing, REST API integration, and interactive visualization to deliver a modern movie recommendation experience inspired by commercial streaming platforms.

Problem Statement

Modern streaming platforms contain thousands of movies across multiple genres and languages. Finding content that genuinely matches a user's interests has become increasingly difficult.

  • Recommendations are often based only on popularity, ignoring niche interests
  • Users receive little explanation for why a movie is being recommended
  • Users frequently switch between IMDb, YouTube, and TMDB to gather info
